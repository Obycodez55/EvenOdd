What are Even Numbers?

Numbers that are completely divisible by 2 are termed as even numbers. These numbers when divided by 2 leave 0 as the remainder.
Even numbers are those numbers that can be divided into two equal groups or pairs and are exactly divisible by 2. For example, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, and so on are even numbers. These numbers can be grouped in equal pairs. However, this grouping cannot be done for numbers like 5, 7, 9, or 11. So, 5, 7, 9, or 11 are not even numbers
An even number is a number that is a multiple of 2. It can be said that any number that is completely divisible by 2 is an even number.

What are Odd Numbers?

A number which is not divisible by “2” is called an odd number. An odd number always ends in 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9. An odd number is always 1 more than (or 1 less than) an even number.Odd numbers are numbers which when divided by 2 leave the remainder 1. In other words, we can say a number, which is not divisible by 2, is an odd number.
